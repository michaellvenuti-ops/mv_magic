The Miracle Manifest

Learning the 52 positions is just the "software" install. These are the "apps" you can run:

Any Card At Any Number (ACAAN) The spectator names a card and a number from 1-52. You don't touch the deck; they deal down, and their card is at that exact position.
The Spelling Trick A spectator chooses a card. You then spell its name (e.g., "S-E-V-E-N-O-F-H-E-A-R-T-S"), dealing one card per letter. The last letter is always their card.
Weight Guessing A spectator cuts a pile of cards. Just by looking at the thickness (or peeking at the bottom card), you can tell them exactly how many cards they are holding.
Poker & Bridge Deals From a stack that looks completely mixed, you can deal a Royal Flush to yourself or a perfect 13-card suit in Bridge.
