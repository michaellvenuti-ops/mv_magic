What is the Mnemonica Stack?

Created by the legendary Spanish magician Juan Tamariz, Mnemonica is a "Memorized Deck." Unlike Si Stebbins, there is no mathematical formula to determine the next card. Instead, you must memorize all 52 cards and their exact positions (1 to 52).

The power of Mnemonica lies in its Built-in Properties. From a seemingly shuffled deck, you can deal a winning Poker hand, a perfect Bridge hand, or perform the "Any Card At Any Number" (ACAAN) miracle instantly because you know the location of every card in the deck at all times.
