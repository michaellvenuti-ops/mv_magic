Intro to Mnemonica

Mnemonica is a "Memorized Deck" created by Juan Tamariz. Unlike Si Stebbins, there is no mathematical formula. You must know every card's exact position (1-52) by heart. This allows for miracles where the cards are never moved, yet you know exactly where everything is.
