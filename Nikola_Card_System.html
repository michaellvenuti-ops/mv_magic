1. System Blueprint & Description

What is the Nikola System?

Unlike Si Stebbins (mathematical) or Mnemonica (pure memory), the Nikola System is a "built-in miracle" stack. It was designed so that specific groups of cards are pre-arranged for massive stage effects while looking entirely random to the audience.

  • The Spelling Bee: The first 15 cards are specifically ordered so that common card names (like "Ace of Spades") can be spelled out, card by card, landing exactly on the selection.
  • The Poker Deal: The stack contains a hidden "slug" that, when dealt in a specific number of hands, results in a Royal Flush in Spades for the performer.
  • The Suit Production: By utilizing Faro shuffles (included in the engine below), you can separate the deck into full suits or colors in minutes.
  • Weight of Cards: Like all stacks, if you know the card at the cut point, you know exactly how many cards were removed.

Note: Because this is a static system, it is highly vulnerable to shuffles—master the Faro Shuffles below to see how the order evolves.
