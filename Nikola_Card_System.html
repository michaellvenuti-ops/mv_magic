1. System Description

Encyclopedia of Card Tricks (Pg. 385)

The Nikola System is a "pre-arranged" stack designed for stage miracles. Unlike the Si Stebbins, it looks completely random but allows for:

  • Poker Demonstrations: Instant Royal Flushes.
  • Spelling Bee: Spell cards directly from the stack.
  • Faro Transformations: Specific shuffles unlock new suit orders.
